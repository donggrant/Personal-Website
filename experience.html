My Work Experience

The Mitre Corporation - Data Science Internship

January 2021 - August 2021

In 2021, I worked at Mitre for both the Winter and Summer seasons. My main accomplishment this time around is creating an automatic annotator for FAA flight data. I improved upon older algorithms that relied heavily on automatic speech recognition. The major weaknesses of such ASR approaches is that most of the mass amount of FAA flight audio is of poor quality. Therefore, by utilizing time stamps and other relational data from non-voice sources like departure and arrival times, and hand-off messages, I was able to significanlty improve the accuracy of the flight annotator, as well as increase its speed and efficiency.Throughout my time working on the annotator, most of my work was done in Python. I managed, manipulated, and analyzed lots of data through HDFS architecture and the PySpark framework

The Mitre Corporation - Software Engineering Internship

May 2020 - August 2020

At Mitre, I worked with a division focusing on the logistics of expanding commercial drone usage and examining the potential risks of extending drone flights into different areas of the country. In my direct line of work, I developed a user interface for their commercial drone risk analysis software using java and its GUI library, and python scripts. During my internship, I worked a lot with big data air traffic files and optimized efficiency and run time of the drone software by connecting it with Mitre’s computer cluster. Overall, my work really helped streamline the process of the drone risk evaluation process and made it more accessible to people of non-technical backgrounds at Mitre.
